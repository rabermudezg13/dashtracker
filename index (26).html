Getting started
The app runs entirely in your browser — no login required, no data is sent to any server. Your settings are saved on your device automatically.
📱 Using the app on your phone:

Copy the app link and send it to yourself on Microsoft Teams. Open it from Teams on your phone — the app works fully in your mobile browser. This is especially useful for scanning documents with your camera on the go.
📊 Tracker 💊 Drug Screen 📷 Scanner

Three tabs at the top give you access to all features. Each section is independent.

1

Open the app link

Use the link shared by Rodrigo. Bookmark it for quick access — it always loads the latest version.

2

Set your name first

Before doing anything else, update your sender info so all templates show your name. See Section 2 for how.

3

Upload your Excel file

Drag your tracker onto the upload area or click to browse. The data is read locally — the file never leaves your device.

2
Setting your name
Your name appears in every email template, SMS template, and the Drug Screen PDF. Set it once — it persists across sessions.
1

Click your name in the top-right corner

You'll see the current name displayed as a small label. Click it to open the sender settings panel.

2

Fill in your details

Your namee.g. John Doe
Your emailjohn.doe@kellyeducation.com
Your titleKelly Education — Miami Dade
Docs emailEmail where talents send documents
3

Click Save & Apply

Your name updates immediately across all templates, the Drug Screen form, and the header. You only need to do this once per device.

3
Uploading your tracker
1

Open your tracker

Open the Miami Dade Tracker from Microsoft Online.

2

Go to File → Create a Copy → Download a Copy

In the menu bar click File, then Create a Copy, then Download a Copy. The file will download as an Excel workbook.

3

Wait about 1 minute

Microsoft Online can take a moment to generate the file. Wait until the download is fully complete before uploading it to the app.

4

Upload to the app

Drag the downloaded file onto the upload area or click to browse. If the workbook has multiple recruiter sheets, a picker screen will appear — tap your name and the app will load only your data.

5

The dashboard loads automatically

No additional clicks needed. The dashboard builds instantly from your data.

Microsoft Online can sometimes be slow to generate the download. If nothing happens, wait a full minute and try again.
To load a new file, click the ↩ New File button in the top-right corner of the header.

6
Email and SMS templates
1

Click a module card to open the talent list

Once the table is visible, a red ✉️ View template button appears in the action bar.

2

Click "View template" to open the modal

Two tabs: Email Template and SMS Template — both pre-filled with your name and the correct module wording.

3

Copy and send

Use Copy email template to copy the body text. The template is ready to paste into any email client.

SMS templates are designed for Sense. You have two options to send them:

Option 1 — Download CSV: Use Download phones CSV to get a Name + Phone file and import it into Sense for bulk messaging.

Option 2 — Grab number: Open Sense directly, copy the phone number from the talent list, and use Grab Number in Sense to send the SMS individually.
7
Using the app on your phone
This app works fully on your mobile browser — no download or install needed. The best way to access it on your phone is through Microsoft Teams.
1

Send the link to yourself on Teams

Copy the app URL and paste it in a Teams message to yourself or your team chat. Open it from Teams on your phone — it loads directly in the mobile browser.

2

Scan documents with your camera

Go to the 📷 Scanner tab. Use Take Photo to open your phone camera and photograph the candidate's documents. Add as many photos as needed — each becomes a page in the PDF.

3

Generate and download the PDF

Tap Generate PDF — the file downloads to your phone with the correct name automatically.

4

Share via Teams

Share the downloaded PDF to yourself via Microsoft Teams. From your desktop, download it from Teams and upload it to the talent's profile in Bullhorn.

🚀 Full mobile workflow at a glance

1Send app link to yourself on Teams → open on phone
2Open Scanner tab → photograph candidate documents
3Generate PDF → downloads to phone with correct filename
4Share PDF via Teams → download on desktop → upload to Bullhorn
8
Drug screen PDF filler
Fills and downloads the Kelly e1141 Oral Instant Swab Drug Screening form automatically. No typing, no printing blanks.
1

Go to the 💊 Drug Screen tab

Your name and branch (W207) are pre-filled from your sender settings. Update Lot # and Expiration Date for the current kit being used.

2

Set the time and date

Click ⏱ Now to auto-fill the current time, and 📅 Today for today's date. You can also type them manually if needed.

3

Download and print

Click ⬇️ Download Filled PDF. The form downloads with all your fields filled in. The yellow-highlighted sections are for the candidate to complete by hand after printing.

Candidate fills in (highlighted in yellow on the printed form)

Candidate name KSN / Last 4 of SSN Candidate signature Date (Step 2)
Digital signatures are not permitted on this form. Both the representative and the candidate must sign by hand after printing.
9
Document scanner
Turns photos of documents into properly named PDFs. Works on mobile — use your camera directly from the app.
1

Select document type

Choose 💊 Drug Screening or 🎒 Education Proof. For education, pick the subtype: Bachelor's, Master's, HS Diploma, Transcripts, Equivalency, etc.

2

Enter the candidate's name

The filename preview updates live as you type, so you can see exactly what the PDF will be named before generating it.

3

Add photos

Use 📷 Take Photo to open your camera directly, or 🖼 Choose from Gallery to pick existing images. You can add multiple photos — each becomes a page in the PDF.

4

Generate and download

Tap Generate PDF — the file downloads automatically with the correct naming format.

5

Share via Teams

Once the PDF downloads to your phone, share it to yourself or your team through Microsoft Teams. From Teams on your desktop, you can then download it and upload it directly to Bullhorn on the talent's profile.

File naming format

DrugScreen_JohnSmith.pdf Education_Bachelors_JohnSmith.pdf Education_HSDiploma_JohnSmith.pdf Education_Transcripts_JohnSmith.pdf Education_Associates_JohnSmith.pdf

Status logic reference

The app uses module-specific rules to determine what counts as "pending." Here is the full reference.

Module Pending when value is... Complete when...
📋 Pre-hire Training Blank, Need Certificate, or any general incomplete value Complete
🦺 Safety & Security Blank, Need Certificate, or any general incomplete value Complete
📝 I-9 Blank, Initiated, or any general incomplete value Complete
💊 Drug Screen Blank, Initiated, or any general incomplete value Complete
🔍 Background Check Blank, Initiated, or any general incomplete value Complete
🖐 FP Screening Anything that is not "Approved" — shows Info Requested label when value is Info Requested Approved
📅 MDPS Training Anything that is not "Pass" (e.g. Scheduled, blank) Pass
🎒 Education Req. Blank or any general incomplete value Any non-blank value (Bachelors, Associates, etc.)
🪪 Badge Photo Blank or general incomplete value Complete, Taken, Printed
General incomplete values recognized across all modules:
blank, No, Pending, N/A, Not Submitted, Incomplete, TBD, Missing, Need Certificate, Initiated, Requested, Will Send, Info Requested, CH Review Pending, Scheduled, Taken.
